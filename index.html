About DailyNodes

DailyNodes helps you build lasting habits by focusing on one day at a time. Watch your daily progress come to life as a beautiful interactive network of connected nodes.

How to Use

  • Tap the menu icon to set your daily intentions
  • Choose a category: Health, Mindfulness, Productivity, Learning, or Relationships
  • Log progress throughout the day by selecting your goal and adding notes
  • Long-press any node to see detailed information
  • Drag nodes to rearrange them on your canvas
  • Tap the center "Today" node to change its color
  • Check the History tab to view your stats and streak

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I delete a goal?
A: In the sidebar, go to "Today's Goals" and tap the X button next to the goal you want to remove.
Q: Can I see my progress from previous days?
A: DailyNodes focuses on today to reduce overwhelm. However, you can view your overall statistics, streak, and total progress in the History tab.
Q: How do I reset node positions?
A: Open the menu, go to the History tab, scroll down to "Reset Options" and tap "Reset Node Positions". Your goals and progress will be kept.
Q: What do the version numbers on small nodes mean?
A: Each small node represents a progress entry. The version number (like "1.0", "2.0") shows which progress entry it is for that goal.
Q: Will my data be saved?
A: Yes! All your goals and progress are automatically saved on your device.
Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?
A: Currently, data is stored locally on each device. Cloud sync is planned for a future update.

Privacy

DailyNodes stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Your goals and progress are private and remain on your device.
