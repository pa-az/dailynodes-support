Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Simple Summary: DailyNodes does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Information Collection and Use

DailyNodes does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. All data you enter into the app (including your goals, progress entries, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

Your data is stored using your device's local storage. This means:

  • No data leaves your device
  • No accounts or sign-ins required
  • No servers or cloud storage involved
  • Your information is completely private

Third-Party Services

DailyNodes does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not integrate with any external services that could collect your data.

Children's Privacy

DailyNodes does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device password-protected.

Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete all data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data from your device
  • No data recovery is possible once deleted, as we do not store backups

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
